📱 Camera Access

⚠️ If permission is denied: Go to Settings → Apps → [Browser Name] → Permissions → Camera → Allow
📍 Android Permission Steps:
  1. Tap "Allow" when prompted
  2. If you tapped "Deny", go to Settings
  3. Apps → Find your browser (Chrome, Firefox, etc.)
  4. Permissions → Camera → Turn ON
✅ Requires camera permission
💡 Works on Chrome, Firefox, Edge (Android)
📸 Photos saved to Downloads/Gallery
